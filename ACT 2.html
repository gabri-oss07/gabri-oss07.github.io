"GABI'S BEST TORTANG TALONG"


Ingredients:

  • Eggplants:4/5 pieces
  • Eggs:3-4 Large eggs
  • Cooking oil:3-4 tablesppons
  • Seasoning:Salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • Optional Aromatics/Fillings:1 small onion (chopped),2 cloves garlic (minced), or ground pork (pre-cooked)

Procedure

  1. Prepare the Eggplant:Grill,broil,or roast the eggplants over an open flame until the skin is charred,blackened,and the flesh is soft.
  2. Peel and Flaten:Allow the eggplants to cool,then peel off the skin,leaving the stem intact.Flatten the eggplant using a fork,spreading it into a wide.
  3. Prepare and Mixture:In a bowl,beat the eggs and season with salt,pepper,and garlic/onions.
  4. Dip and Fry:Dip the flattened eggplant into the egg mixture.Heat oil in pan over medium heat and place the eggplant into the hot oil.
  5. Cook:Pour any remaining egg mixture over the eggplant.Fry until the bottom is golden brown then carefully flip to cook the other side.
  6. Serve:Remove from the pan,drain on paper towels,and serve hot with rice and banana ketchup.
